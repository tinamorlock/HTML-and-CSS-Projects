Full Stack Books Process for Authors

  1. Submit your manuscript to our managing editor.
  2. A team of readers who are experts in your genre will beta read your manuscript.
  3. Managing editor will send you in-depth feedback.
  4. You can resubmit your manuscript (if not accepted) once you revise.

Why Authors Love Full Stack Books

  • They earn more than with a traditional publisher.
  • They don't have to do their own marketing.
  • They connect with more readers who love their stories.
